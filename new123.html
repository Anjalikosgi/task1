Introduction

Literacy is the foundation of lifelong learning. This program bridges the gap between "learning to read" and "reading to learn".

The Core Challenge

  • Focus on decoding over comprehension
  • Lack of reading motivation
  • Limited exposure to diverse texts
  • Passive learning environments
  • Inadequate support for varied reading levels
  • Neglect of socio-emotional aspects

Program Methodology

Combines phonemic awareness, phonics, fluency with comprehension strategies like predicting, summarizing, and visualizing.

Students choose reading materials suited to their interest and level. Platforms track progress and personalize next steps.

Includes book clubs, peer reading, shared discussions, and feedback that make reading interactive and meaningful.
